📌 WHY IN NEWS
The Union Minister, Shri Manohar Lal, chaired a meeting of the Parliamentary Consultative Committee to review the performance of NBCC and CPWD.

📚 GS2 — Governance: Government Schemes and Initiatives

The Minister of Housing and Urban Affairs reviewed the progress of various projects being implemented by NBCC and CPWD, emphasizing the need for timely completion and quality standards. The meeting also discussed the issue of delayed payments to contractors.

✅ KEY FACTS
• Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs — Review Meeting: The Minister reviewed the progress of various projects being implemented by NBCC and CPWD.
• NBCC — Project Implementation: The agency is implementing several projects, including the redevelopment of government colonies and the construction of office buildings.
• CPWD — Project Implementation: The agency is also implementing various infrastructure projects, including the construction of roads and bridges.
