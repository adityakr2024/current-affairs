📚 GS2 — Governance: Government Schemes and Initiatives

The Centre has set conditions for states to receive Jal Jeevan Mission funds, aiming to improve water supply and sanitation across the country. The mission aims to cover 19.40 crore rural households, with a focus on marginalized communities.

✅ KEY FACTS
• Ministry of Jal Shakti — Jal Jeevan Mission: ₹1.42 lakh crore allocated for the mission.
• Ministry of Jal Shakti — Jal Jeevan Mission: 19.40 crore rural households to be covered.
• State Governments — State Water Authority: formation of authority mandatory for receiving funds.
