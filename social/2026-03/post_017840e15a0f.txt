📌 WHY IN NEWS
The Centre has set conditions for states to receive funds under the Jal Jeevan Mission, a scheme aimed at providing piped water to all households by 2026.

📚 GS2 — Social Justice: Drinking Water and Sanitation

The Centre has set conditions for states to receive funds under the Jal Jeevan Mission, including the formation of a State Water Authority and a State Level Scheme Sanctioning Committee. The mission aims to provide piped water to all households by 2026, with ₹2.5 lakh crore in funding over five years.

✅ KEY FACTS
• Ministry of Jal Shakti — Jal Jeevan Mission: Providing ₹2.5 lakh crore in funding over five years.
• State Governments — Jal Jeevan Mission: Required to form a State Water Authority and a State Level Scheme Sanctioning Committee.
• Ministry of Jal Shakti — Jal Jeevan Mission: Aims to cover 19.13 crore rural households by 2026.
