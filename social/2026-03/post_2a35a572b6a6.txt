📌 WHY IN NEWS
Kerala Institute of Local Administration (KILA) has signed a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) with the United Nations University (UNU) research institute, UNU-CRIS, to enhance research, policy support, and capacity building in local governance.

📚 GS2 — Governance: Government Schemes and Initiatives

KILA has signed an MoU with UNU-CRIS to boost research, policy support, and capacity building in local governance. This partnership will focus on key areas such as decentralization, urban governance, and sustainable development, ultimately benefiting the citizens of Kerala.

✅ KEY FACTS
• KILA — MoU with UNU-CRIS: Partnership to enhance research, policy support, and capacity building in local governance.
• KILA — Capacity Building: Focus on decentralization, urban governance, and sustainable development.
• UNU-CRIS — Research: Collaborative research initiatives to support local governance.
