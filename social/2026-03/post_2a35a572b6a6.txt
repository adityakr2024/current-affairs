📚 GS2 — Governance: Government Schemes and Initiatives

Kerala Institute of Local Administration (KILA) has signed an MoU with the United Nations University Institute on Comparative Regional Integration Studies (UNU-CRIS) to boost research and policy support for local governance. This partnership will strengthen local governments and improve their service delivery.

✅ KEY FACTS
• Kerala Institute of Local Administration (KILA) — MoU with UNU-CRIS: Partnership to enhance research, policy support, and capacity building in local governance.
• UNU-CRIS — Research Focus: Comparative regional integration studies to support local governance.
• KILA — Capacity Building: Training programs for local government officials to improve service delivery.
