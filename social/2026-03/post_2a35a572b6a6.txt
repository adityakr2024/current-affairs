KILA signs MoU with UN University research institute to boost research, policy support, and capacity building in local governance.

📚 GS2 — Governance: Government Schemes and Initiatives

KILA has signed an MoU with UNU-CRIS to enhance research, policy support, and capacity building in local governance. This partnership aims to promote sustainable development and decentralization in Kerala.

✅ KEY FACTS
• KILA — MoU with UNU-CRIS: Partnership to enhance research, policy support, and capacity building in local governance.
• KILA — Training Programs: Focus on sustainable development, decentralization, and local self-governance.
• UNU-CRIS — Research Focus: Comparative regional integration studies, sustainable development, and local governance.
