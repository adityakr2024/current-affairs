📚 GS2 — Governance: Government Schemes and Initiatives

Kerala Institute of Local Administration (KILA) has signed an MoU with the United Nations University (UNU) research institute to boost research and policy support in local governance. This partnership aims to strengthen local governments in Kerala and beyond, promoting effective and sustainable governance practices.

✅ KEY FACTS
• KILA — MoU with UNU-CRIS: Partnership to boost research and policy support in local governance.
• UNU-CRIS — Research Focus: Decentralization, urban governance, and sustainable development.
• KILA — Capacity Building: Initiatives to enhance the capacity of local governments in Kerala and beyond.
