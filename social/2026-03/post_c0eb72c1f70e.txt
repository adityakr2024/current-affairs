📌 WHY IN NEWS
The Supreme Court has ruled that parental income alone cannot decide OBC creamy layer status, in a decision that favours children of senior PSU officials selected as OBC candidates in civil service exams.

📚 GS2 — Social Justice: Other Backward Classes and Reservation Policy

The Supreme Court has ruled that parental income alone cannot decide OBC creamy layer status. This decision is expected to impact the selection process for civil service exams and other government jobs, benefiting children of senior PSU officials who were selected as OBC candidates.

✅ KEY FACTS
• Supreme Court — OBC Creamy Layer: Rules that parental income alone cannot decide creamy layer status.
• Supreme Court — Civil Service Exams: Favouring children of senior PSU officials selected as OBC candidates.
• Government — Creamy Layer Exclusion Framework: Income from salaries and farm land cannot be included for wealth test.
