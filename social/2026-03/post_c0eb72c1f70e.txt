📌 WHY IN NEWS
The Supreme Court has ruled that parental income alone cannot decide the OBC creamy layer status, in a decision that favours children of senior PSU officials selected as OBC candidates in civil service exams.

📚 GS2 — Social Justice: Reservation and Creamy Layer

The Supreme Court has ruled that parental income alone cannot decide the OBC creamy layer status. This decision will impact the eligibility of OBC candidates for government jobs and education. The Court's ruling will help to ensure that the benefits of reservation are targeted towards those who need them most.

✅ KEY FACTS
• Supreme Court — OBC Creamy Layer: Rules that parental income alone cannot decide creamy layer status.
• Supreme Court — Civil Service Exams: Favouring children of senior PSU officials selected as OBC candidates.
• Government — Creamy Layer Exclusion Framework: Income from salaries and farm land cannot be included for wealth test.
