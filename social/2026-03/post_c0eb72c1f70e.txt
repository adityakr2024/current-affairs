📌 WHY IN NEWS
The Supreme Court has ruled that parental income alone cannot decide the OBC creamy layer status of a candidate, in a judgment that favours children of senior PSU officials selected as OBC candidates in civil service exams.

📚 GS2 — Polity: Reservation and Affirmative Action

The Supreme Court has ruled that parental income alone cannot decide the OBC creamy layer status of a candidate. This judgment is expected to impact the selection process for civil service exams. The court's decision will help ensure that reservations are availed by deserving candidates.

✅ KEY FACTS
• Supreme Court — OBC Creamy Layer: Parental income alone cannot decide creamy layer status.
• Supreme Court — Civil Service Exams: Children of senior PSU officials selected as OBC candidates are eligible.
• Government — Creamy Layer Exclusion Framework: Income from salaries and farm land cannot be included for wealth test.
