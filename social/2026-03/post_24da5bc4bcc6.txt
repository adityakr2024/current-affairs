📌 WHY IN NEWS
The Kerala Cabinet has approved the formulation of land assignment rules for the use of pattaya land for other purposes, including waiving fees for specific types of constructions.

📚 GS2 — Governance: Government Schemes and Initiatives

The Kerala Cabinet has approved the formulation of land assignment rules to allow the use of pattaya land for purposes other than agriculture. Fees will be waived for specific types of constructions, including houses, buildings for agricultural and educational purposes.

✅ KEY FACTS
• Kerala Cabinet — Land Assignment Rules: Approved formulation of rules for use of pattaya land for other purposes.
• Kerala Government — Fee Waiver: Fees will be waived for houses and buildings constructed for agricultural and educational purposes.
• Kerala Government — Fee Waiver: Fees will also be waived for markets, places of worship, structures for public purposes, cultural institutions, clubs, and charitable establishments.
