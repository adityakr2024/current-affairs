📚 GS2 — Governance: Government Schemes and Initiatives

The Kerala Cabinet has approved the formulation of land assignment rules to allow the use of pattaya land for other purposes, including waiving fees for specific constructions. This move is expected to benefit various sectors, including housing, agriculture, education, and public services.

✅ KEY FACTS
• Kerala Cabinet — Land Assignment Rules: Approved formulation for use of pattaya land for other purposes.
• Kerala Government — Fee Waiver: Fees will be waived for houses and specific constructions for public purposes.
• Kerala Government — Eligible Constructions: Includes buildings for agricultural, educational, and charitable purposes.
