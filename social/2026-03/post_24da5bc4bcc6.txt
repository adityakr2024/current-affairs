📚 GS2 — Governance: Government Schemes and Initiatives

The Kerala Cabinet has approved the formulation of land assignment rules for the use of pattaya land for other purposes, including waiving fees for houses and buildings constructed for agricultural, educational, and public purposes. This move is expected to promote development and boost economic growth in the region.

✅ KEY FACTS
• Kerala Cabinet — Land Assignment Rules: Formulation of rules for the use of pattaya land for other purposes.
• Kerala Government — Fee Waiver: Fees will be waived for houses and buildings constructed for agricultural, educational, and public purposes.
• Kerala Cabinet — Land Use: Pattaya land can be used for constructing houses, buildings for agricultural purposes, educational institutions, markets, places of worship, cultural institutions, and charitable establishments.
