📌 WHY IN NEWS
The Kerala Cabinet has approved the formulation of land assignment rules for the use of pattaya land for other purposes, including waiving fees for houses and buildings constructed for agricultural and educational purposes.

📚 GS2 — Governance: Government Schemes and Initiatives

The Kerala Cabinet has approved the formulation of land assignment rules for the use of pattaya land for other purposes. Fees will be waived for houses and buildings constructed for agricultural and educational purposes. This decision is expected to benefit various sectors and promote development in the state.

✅ KEY FACTS
• Kerala Cabinet — Land Assignment Rules: Formulation of rules for use of pattaya land for other purposes.
• Kerala Government — Fee Waiver: Waiving fees for houses and buildings constructed for agricultural and educational purposes.
• Kerala Government — Land Use: Allowing use of pattaya land for markets, places of worship, structures for public purposes, cultural institutions, clubs and structures for charitable establishments.
