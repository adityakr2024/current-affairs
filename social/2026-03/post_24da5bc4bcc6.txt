📚 GS2 — Governance: Government Schemes and Initiatives

The Kerala Cabinet has approved the formulation of land assignment rules to allow the use of pattaya land for other purposes, including waiving fees for specific constructions. This move is expected to facilitate development and utilization of pattaya land in a more efficient manner.

✅ KEY FACTS
• Kerala Cabinet — Land Assignment Rules: Formulation approved for use of pattaya land for other purposes.
• Kerala Government — Fee Waiver: Fees waived for houses and buildings constructed for agricultural and educational purposes.
• Kerala Government — Fee Waiver: Fees also waived for markets, places of worship, and structures for public purposes.
