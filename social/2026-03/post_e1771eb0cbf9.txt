📚 GS2 — Governance: Government Schemes and Initiatives

The Kerala CM is set to lay the foundation stone for the ₹3,509cr Marine Eco City project in Kochi, which aims to transform the city's landscape and provide amenities to residents. The project is expected to bring substantial changes to the area and contribute to the city's growth and development.

✅ KEY FACTS
• Kerala Government — Marine Eco City project: Laying foundation stone for Phase 1 with a total cost of ₹3,509 crore.
• Kerala Government — Marine Eco City project: Aiming to transform Kochi's landscape and provide amenities to residents.
• Kerala Government — Marine Eco City project: Focusing on sustainability and environmental conservation.
