📚 GS2 — International Relations: Iran-U.S.-Israel Relations

Iran has announced that it will target U.S.-Israeli economic and banking interests in the region after an overnight attack on Iranian Bank Sepah in Tehran. The move is expected to escalate tensions between Iran, the U.S., and Israel.

✅ KEY FACTS
• Iranian government — Economic Sanctions: Imposing retaliatory measures against U.S.-Israeli economic interests.
• U.S. government — Economic Sanctions: Imposed sanctions on Iran in 2018, affecting its banking and financial sector.
• Iranian Bank Sepah — Attack: Hit overnight in Tehran, raising concerns about escalating tensions.
