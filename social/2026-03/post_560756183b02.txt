📌 WHY IN NEWS
The Prime Minister of India, Narendra Modi, has inaugurated an elevated port corridor in Guwahati and launched multiple waterways projects with an investment of ₹526 crore in Assam.

📚 GS3 — Economy: Infrastructure Development and Transportation

The Prime Minister has launched multiple waterways projects in Assam with an investment of ₹526 crore, expected to create new job opportunities and stimulate economic growth in the region. The projects include the development of jetties, terminals, and other infrastructure to support the growth of the waterways sector.

✅ KEY FACTS
• The Prime Minister — Elevated Port Corridor: Inaugurated in Guwahati with an investment of ₹526 crore.
• The Government — Waterways Projects: Launched multiple projects to develop jetties, terminals, and other infrastructure.
• The Ministry of Shipping — National Waterways: Plans to increase the length of national waterways in Assam from 891 km to 1,158 km.
