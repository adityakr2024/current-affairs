📌 WHY IN NEWS
The Supreme Court has passed an order regarding the payment of sugarcane farmers, prompting the sugar mill association to welcome the decision, which affects 5 crore farmers and ₹50,000 crore in annual payments.

📚 GS2: Polity & Governance: Judiciary

The Supreme Court has ordered sugar mills to pay farmers within 14 days of purchase at a rate of ₹275 per quintal, affecting 5 crore farmers and ₹50,000 crore in annual payments. This decision is expected to improve the financial stability of farmers and increase investment in agriculture, with potential implications for the entire agricultural sector.

✅ KEY FACTS
• The Supreme Court — Sugarcane Payment Order: Directed sugar mills to pay farmers within 14 days of purchase.
• The Supreme Court — Sugarcane Payment Order: Set the payment rate at ₹275 per quintal.
• Ministry of Consumer Affairs, Food and Public Distribution — Oversight: Will work with state governments to implement the order.
