📌 WHY IN NEWS
The Supreme Court has passed an order that has been welcomed by the sugar mill association, bringing relief to the industry.

📚 GS3 — Economy: Agriculture and Allied Sectors

The Supreme Court has ordered the release of ₹62,000 crore in export subsidies for sugar mills, bringing relief to the industry. This move is expected to benefit over 5,000 sugar mills across the country.

✅ KEY FACTS
• The Supreme Court — Export Subsidy: Ordered the release of ₹62,000 crore for sugar mills.
• The Supreme Court — Export Subsidy: Subsidy to be disbursed over two years, with ₹30,000 crore in the first year.
• The Supreme Court — Export Subsidy: ₹32,000 crore to be released in the second year.
