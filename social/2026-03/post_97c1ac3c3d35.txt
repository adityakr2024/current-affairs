📌 WHY IN NEWS
The Supreme Court has passed an order that has been welcomed by the sugar mill association, bringing relief to the industry.

📚 GS3 — Economy: Agriculture and Allied Activities

The Supreme Court has allowed sugar mills to sell their excess sugar stock, bringing relief to the industry. This move is expected to benefit around 500 sugar mills and stabilize the market.

✅ KEY FACTS
• Supreme Court — Excess Sugar Sale: Allowed sugar mills to sell their excess sugar stock.
• Sugar Mill Association — Relief: Welcomed the Supreme Court order, citing relief for the industry.
• Indian Sugar Industry — Annual Turnover: ₹1.5 lakh crore.
