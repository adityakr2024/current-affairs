📌 WHY IN NEWS
The Supreme Court has passed an order regarding the sugar industry, prompting a welcome response from the sugar mill association.

📚 GS2 — Governance: Government Schemes and Initiatives

The Supreme Court has directed sugar mills to clear pending dues of ₹5,000 crore to sugarcane farmers, providing relief to over 5 million farmers. The order is expected to boost the sugar industry, which has been facing financial difficulties.

✅ KEY FACTS
• Supreme Court — Order: Directed sugar mills to clear pending dues of ₹5,000 crore to sugarcane farmers.
• Sugar Mill Association — Reaction: Welcomed the Supreme Court's order, stating it will provide relief to the industry.
• Sugarcane Farmers — Benefit: Over 5 million farmers are expected to benefit from the order, with payments to be made within a specified timeframe.
