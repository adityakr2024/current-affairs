📌 WHY IN NEWS
The Supreme Court has passed an order regarding the sugar industry, prompting a positive response from the sugar mill association.

📚 GS3 — Economy: Agriculture and Allied Sectors

The Supreme Court has ordered sugar mills to pay dues to sugarcane farmers, with the total amount owed estimated to be around ₹2.5 lakh crore. This move is expected to benefit over 5 million sugarcane farmers across the country.

✅ KEY FACTS
• Supreme Court — Sugar Industry Order: Directed sugar mills to clear outstanding payments to sugarcane farmers.
• Sugar Mill Association — Reaction: Welcomed the Supreme Court's order, citing it as a respite for the industry.
• Sugarcane Farmers — Benefit: Expected to benefit over 5 million sugarcane farmers across the country.
