📌 WHY IN NEWS
The Supreme Court has passed an order that has been welcomed by the sugar mill association.

📚 GS2 — Governance: Government Schemes and Initiatives

The Supreme Court has ordered sugar mills to pay ₹6,300 crore in dues to farmers. This move is expected to benefit 5 million farmers who were awaiting payment.

✅ KEY FACTS
• Supreme Court — Order: Directed sugar mills to pay ₹6,300 crore in dues to farmers.
• Sugar Mill Association — Statement: Welcomed the order, stating it will help resolve the issue.
• Supreme Court — Order: Affects 5 million farmers who were awaiting payment.
