The Supreme Court has passed an order that brings relief to the sugar industry, with over 500 mills benefiting from the decision. The industry is expected to see an increase in production and employment, with a total investment of ₹20,000 crore.

✅ KEY FACTS
• Supreme Court — Order: Relief to sugar industry with ₹20,000 crore investment.
• Sugar Mill Association — Reaction: Welcomed the order, expecting increased sugar production.
• Sugar Industry — Impact: Benefits over 500 sugar mills, providing employment to thousands of workers.
