📌 WHY IN NEWS
The Supreme Court has passed an order regarding the sugar industry, prompting a welcome response from the sugar mill association.

📚 GS2 — Governance: Government Schemes and Initiatives

The Supreme Court has ruled in favor of sugarcane farmers, ensuring they receive fair payment for their produce. This decision is expected to impact approximately 50 million farmers, with payments totaling ₹60,000 crore.

✅ KEY FACTS
• Supreme Court — Sugarcane Payment Order: Ruled in favor of sugarcane farmers, ensuring payment of dues.
• Sugar Mill Association — Industry Stability: Welcomed the order, citing the need for a stable and fair pricing system.
• Government of India — Sugar Industry Support: Expected to provide support to maintain a viable sugar industry.
