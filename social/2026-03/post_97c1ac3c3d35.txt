📌 WHY IN NEWS
The Supreme Court has passed an order regarding the sugar industry, which has been welcomed by the sugar mill association.

📚 GS2 — Governance: Government Schemes and Initiatives

The Supreme Court has ordered sugar mills to pay ₹45,000 crore in dues to sugarcane farmers. This move is expected to benefit over 5 million farmers across the country.

✅ KEY FACTS
• Supreme Court — Order: Directed sugar mills to pay ₹45,000 crore in dues to sugarcane farmers.
• Sugar Mill Association — Reaction: Welcomed the Supreme Court order, citing clarity on payment schedule.
• Farmers — Benefit: Over 5 million farmers expected to benefit from the payment of dues.
