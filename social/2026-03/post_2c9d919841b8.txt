Thrissur district has achieved full housing coverage under the LIFE Mission, ensuring housing support for all eligible beneficiaries in the homeless-with-land category.

📚 GS2 — Governance: Government Schemes and Initiatives

Thrissur district in Kerala has become the first to achieve full housing coverage under the LIFE Mission, providing housing support to all eligible beneficiaries in the homeless-with-land category. This milestone demonstrates the effectiveness of the mission in providing housing support to those in need.

✅ KEY FACTS
• Kerala Government — LIFE Mission: Achieved full housing coverage in Thrissur district.
• Kerala Government — LIFE Mission: Provided housing support to all eligible beneficiaries in the homeless-with-land category.
• Kerala Government — LIFE Mission:Allocated significant funds for the implementation of the mission.
