📌 WHY IN NEWS
The Thrissur district has achieved full housing coverage under the LIFE Mission, becoming the first district to ensure housing support for all eligible beneficiaries in the homeless-with-land category.

📚 GS2 — Governance: Government Schemes and Initiatives

Thrissur district has become the first to achieve full housing coverage under the LIFE Mission, with 14,359 beneficiaries receiving housing support. This is a significant milestone in the state's housing policy.

✅ KEY FACTS
• The Kerala government — LIFE Mission: Provided housing support to 14,359 beneficiaries in Thrissur district.
• The Thrissur district — LIFE Mission: Achieved full housing coverage, becoming the first district to do so.
• The LIFE Mission — Housing Support: Aims to provide housing support to all eligible beneficiaries in Kerala.
