📌 WHY IN NEWS
The government has announced that the next instalment of the Kisan Nidhi scheme will be disbursed to farmers on March 13.

📚 GS2 — Governance: Government Schemes and Initiatives

The 22nd instalment of the Kisan Nidhi scheme will be released on March 13, supporting over 2.15 crore women farmers. This initiative aims to empower women in agriculture and boost the agricultural sector.

✅ KEY FACTS
• Agriculture Ministry — Kisan Nidhi: 22nd instalment to be released on March 13.
• Agriculture Ministry — Kisan Nidhi: Over 2.15 crore women farmers to receive financial assistance.
• Agriculture Ministry — Kisan Nidhi: Scheme aims to support women in agriculture.
