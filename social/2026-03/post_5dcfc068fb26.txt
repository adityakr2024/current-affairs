📌 WHY IN NEWS
The government has announced that the next instalment of the Kisan Nidhi scheme will be disbursed to farmers on March 13.

📚 GS2 — Governance: Government Schemes and Initiatives

The government has announced that the next instalment of the Kisan Nidhi scheme will be released on March 13, with over 2.15 crore women farmers set to receive financial assistance. This move is expected to have a positive impact on the agricultural sector.

✅ KEY FACTS
• The Ministry of Agriculture — Kisan Nidhi: 22nd instalment to be released on March 13.
• The Ministry of Agriculture — Kisan Nidhi: Over 2.15 crore women farmers to receive financial assistance.
• The Ministry of Agriculture — Kisan Nidhi: Scheme aims to provide financial support to farmers across the country.
