📌 WHY IN NEWS
The government has announced that the next instalment of the Kisan Nidhi scheme will be released on March 13, benefiting over 2.15 crore women farmers.

📚 GS2 — Governance: Government Schemes and Initiatives

The next instalment of the Kisan Nidhi scheme will be released on March 13, benefiting over 2.15 crore women farmers. This scheme aims to provide a minimum income guarantee to small and marginal farmers.

✅ KEY FACTS
• Agriculture Ministry — Kisan Nidhi: 22nd instalment to be released on March 13.
• Agriculture Ministry — Kisan Nidhi: Over 2.15 crore women farmers to receive financial assistance.
• Agriculture Ministry — Kisan Nidhi: Scheme aims to provide minimum income guarantee to small and marginal farmers.
