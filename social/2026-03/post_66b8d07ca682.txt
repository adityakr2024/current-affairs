📚 GS3 — Economy: Monetary Policy and RBI

The RBI has conducted a purchase of government securities worth ₹50,000 crore to inject liquidity into the market. This move comes as the rupee sank to a record low intra-day due to surging crude oil prices.

✅ KEY FACTS
• RBI — G-Secs Purchase: ₹50,000 crore to inject liquidity into the market.
• RBI — G-Secs Purchase: Another tranche scheduled for Friday.
• RBI — Monetary Policy: Aimed at stabilizing the currency and maintaining liquidity.
