📌 WHY IN NEWS
ISRO and AIIMS have signed a memorandum of understanding for cooperation in space medicine and research.

📚 GS3 — Science & Tech: Space Technology and ISRO

ISRO and AIIMS have signed an MoU to promote joint research in space medicine and other areas, aiming to advance human health and performance during space missions. This collaboration will enhance the capabilities of both institutions in space research and medicine.

✅ KEY FACTS
• ISRO — MoU with AIIMS: Cooperation in space medicine and research.
• ISRO — Priority Areas: Joint research aligned with ISRO's priority areas.
• AIIMS — Research Collaboration: Partnership to advance human health and performance.
