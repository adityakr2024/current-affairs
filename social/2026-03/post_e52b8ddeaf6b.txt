📌 WHY IN NEWS
ISRO and AIIMS have signed a memorandum of understanding (MoU) for cooperation in space medicine and research.

📚 GS3 — Science & Tech: Space Technology and ISRO

ISRO and AIIMS have signed an MoU to promote joint research in space medicine and research. The collaboration aims to advance human health, performance, and safety during space missions.

✅ KEY FACTS
• ISRO — Space Medicine Research: Joint research with AIIMS to advance human health and safety during space missions.
• ISRO — Gaganyaan Mission: Aims to launch its first manned mission by 2023.
• AIIMS — Space Medicine Research: Collaboration with ISRO to promote research in space medicine.
