📌 WHY IN NEWS
ISRO and AIIMS have signed a memorandum of understanding (MoU) for cooperation in space medicine and research.

📚 GS3 — Science & Tech: Space Technology and ISRO

ISRO and AIIMS have signed an MoU to promote joint research in space medicine and related areas. This collaboration aims to advance human health and performance in space, supporting ISRO's priority areas and the Gaganyaan programme.

✅ KEY FACTS
• ISRO — Space Medicine Research: Collaboration with AIIMS to advance human health and performance in space.
• AIIMS — Medical Research: Partnership with ISRO to contribute to the development of space medicine and related areas.
• ISRO — Gaganyaan Programme: MoU aims to support the programme's objectives, including astronaut health and safety.
