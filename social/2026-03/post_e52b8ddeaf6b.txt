📌 WHY IN NEWS
The Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO) and the All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS) have signed a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) for cooperation in space medicine and research.

📚 GS3 — Science & Tech: Space Technology and ISRO

ISRO and AIIMS have signed an MoU to promote joint research in space medicine and research. The collaboration will enable the sharing of expertise and resources, leading to advancements in human health and safety during space missions.

✅ KEY FACTS
• ISRO — Space Medicine Research: Joint research with AIIMS to advance human health and safety during space missions.
• AIIMS — Space Medicine Research: Collaboration with ISRO to share expertise and resources in space medicine.
• ISRO — Human Spaceflight Missions: Priority areas for joint research include advancing human health and performance during space missions.
