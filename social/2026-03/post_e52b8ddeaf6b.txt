📌 WHY IN NEWS
ISRO and AIIMS have signed a memorandum of understanding for cooperation in space medicine and research.

📚 GS3 — Science & Tech: Space Technology and ISRO

ISRO and AIIMS have signed an MoU to promote joint research in space medicine, aiming to advance human health and performance in space. This collaboration will contribute to the success of India's human spaceflight missions.

✅ KEY FACTS
• ISRO — Space Medicine Research: Collaboration with AIIMS to advance human health and performance in space.
• AIIMS — Medical Research: Partnership with ISRO to contribute to the success of human spaceflight missions.
• ISRO — Gaganyaan Programme: Expected to benefit from the collaboration with AIIMS in the field of space medicine.
