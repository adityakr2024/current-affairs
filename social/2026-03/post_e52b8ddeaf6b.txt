📌 WHY IN NEWS
ISRO and AIIMS have signed a memorandum of understanding (MoU) for cooperation in space medicine and research.

📚 GS3 — Science & Tech: Space Technology and ISRO

ISRO and AIIMS have signed an MoU to promote joint research in space medicine and related areas. This collaboration is expected to enhance the capabilities of both institutions in the field of space medicine and research, contributing to the success of human spaceflight missions.

✅ KEY FACTS
• ISRO — Space Medicine Research: Collaboration with AIIMS to advance human health and performance in space.
• AIIMS — Medical Research: Partnership with ISRO to enhance capabilities in space medicine and related areas.
• ISRO — Gaganyaan Programme: MoU expected to contribute to the success of human spaceflight missions.
