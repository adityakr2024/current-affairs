📌 WHY IN NEWS
The Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO) signed a memorandum of understanding (MoU) with the All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS) to promote cooperation in space medicine and research on a specific date.

📚 GS3 — Science & Technology: Space Technology and ISRO

ISRO has signed an MoU with AIIMS to advance human health and safety in space missions. This partnership will facilitate joint research in space medicine and related areas, enhancing India's capabilities in human spaceflight.

✅ KEY FACTS
• ISRO — Space Medicine Research: Partnership with AIIMS to advance human health and safety in space missions.
• ISRO — Joint Research: Collaboration with AIIMS in areas aligned with ISRO's priority sectors.
• AIIMS — Space Medicine Expertise: Sharing of expertise and resources with ISRO for human spaceflight missions.
