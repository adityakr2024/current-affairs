📌 WHY IN NEWS
ISRO and AIIMS have signed a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) for cooperation in space medicine and research.

📚 GS3 — Science & Tech: Space Technology and ISRO

ISRO and AIIMS have signed an MoU to promote joint research in space medicine and research. The collaboration aims to advance human health, performance, and safety during space missions. This partnership will contribute to the growth of India's space program and help achieve its goals in space technology.

✅ KEY FACTS
• ISRO — MoU: Signed with AIIMS for cooperation in space medicine and research.
• ISRO — Research: Focus on priority areas, including human health and performance during space missions.
• AIIMS — Collaboration: Partnering with ISRO to advance research in space medicine.
