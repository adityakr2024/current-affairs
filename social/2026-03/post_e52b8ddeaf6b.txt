📌 WHY IN NEWS
The Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO) and the All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS) have signed a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) for cooperation in space medicine and research.

📚 GS3 — Science & Tech: Space Technology and ISRO

ISRO and AIIMS have signed an MoU to advance human health and safety in space missions. This collaboration will enable the development of specialized medical care and research for space travelers.

✅ KEY FACTS
• ISRO — Space Medicine Research: Joint research with AIIMS to advance human health and safety in space missions.
• AIIMS — Medical Research: Collaboration with ISRO to develop specialized medical care for space travelers.
• ISRO — Human Spaceflight: Priority area for joint research with AIIMS.
