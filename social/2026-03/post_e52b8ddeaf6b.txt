📌 WHY IN NEWS
The Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO) and the All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS) have signed a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) for cooperation in space medicine and research.

📚 GS3 — Science & Tech: Space Technology and ISRO

ISRO and AIIMS have signed an MoU to advance human health and safety in space missions. The collaboration will facilitate joint research projects and sharing of expertise and resources.

✅ KEY FACTS
• ISRO — MoU: Signed with AIIMS for cooperation in space medicine and research.
• ISRO — Gaganyaan Mission: Aims to launch its first manned mission in the near future.
• AIIMS — Research: Will collaborate with ISRO on joint research projects.
