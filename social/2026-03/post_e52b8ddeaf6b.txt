ISRO and AIIMS signed a memorandum of understanding for cooperation in space medicine and research.

📚 GS3 — Science & Tech: Space Technology and ISRO

ISRO and AIIMS have signed an MoU to promote joint research in space medicine and related areas. This cooperation is expected to enhance the capabilities of both institutions in the field of space medicine and research, with potential applications in future human spaceflight missions.

✅ KEY FACTS
• ISRO — MoU: Signed with AIIMS for cooperation in space medicine and research.
• ISRO — Gaganyaan programme: Aims to develop capabilities for sustained human presence in space.
• AIIMS — Research: To focus on advancing human health, performance, and safety during human space missions.
