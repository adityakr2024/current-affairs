📌 WHY IN NEWS
The Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO) and the All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS) signed a memorandum of understanding (MoU) for cooperation in space medicine and research.

📚 GS3 — Science & Tech: Space Technology and ISRO

ISRO and AIIMS have signed an MoU to promote joint research in space medicine and research. The collaboration aims to advance human health, performance, and safety during human space missions.

✅ KEY FACTS
• ISRO — Space Medicine Research: Joint research with AIIMS to advance human health in space missions.
• AIIMS — Space Medicine Research: Collaboration with ISRO to improve performance and safety in human space missions.
• ISRO — Gaganyaan Mission: Aim to launch the first manned mission by 2023.
