📌 WHY IN NEWS
The Prime Minister inaugurated various development projects in Kokrajhar, Assam via video conferencing.

📚 GS2 — Governance: Government Schemes and Initiatives

The Prime Minister has inaugurated several development projects in Kokrajhar, Assam, including a medical college, law college, and infrastructure development schemes, with a total outlay of ₹500 crore. These initiatives aim to enhance education, healthcare, and connectivity in the region, benefiting over 1 lakh people.

✅ KEY FACTS
• Ministry of Development of North Eastern Region — Infrastructure Development Schemes: ₹300 crore allocated for road construction and renovation.
• Ministry of Health and Family Welfare — Medical College: ₹150 crore sanctioned for the establishment of a new medical college in Kokrajhar.
• Ministry of Law and Justice — Law College: ₹50 crore approved for the construction of a law college in the region.
