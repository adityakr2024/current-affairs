📚 GS2 — Social Justice: Scheduled Tribes and Forest Rights

A house panel has expressed disappointment over the slow progress in executing flagship schemes for tribal villages, citing deep mismanagement. The schemes aim to improve the lives of tribal communities through infrastructure development, education, and healthcare.

✅ KEY FACTS
• House Panel — Flagship Schemes: Expressed disappointment over slow progress in execution.
• Line Ministries — Scheme Implementation: Lack of coordination and funds allocated for schemes.
• Tribal Villages — Development Initiatives: Include development of infrastructure, education, and healthcare.
