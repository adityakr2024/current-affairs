The Andhra Pradesh government has credited ₹2,670 crore to the accounts of 46.85 lakh farmers as the third instalment of the PM Kisan-Annadata Sukhibhava programme.

📚 GS3 — Economy: Agriculture and Rural Development

The Andhra Pradesh government has credited ₹2,670 crore to 46.85 lakh farmers under the PM Kisan-Annadata Sukhibhava programme. This initiative aims to provide financial assistance to farmers and improve their income.

✅ KEY FACTS
• Andhra Pradesh government — PM Kisan-Annadata Sukhibhava programme: ₹2,670 crore credited to 46.85 lakh farmers.
• State government — PM Kisan-Annadata Sukhibhava programme: ₹1,874 crore contributed as the State's share.
• PM Kisan-Annadata Sukhibhava programme: aims to provide financial assistance to farmers and improve their income.
