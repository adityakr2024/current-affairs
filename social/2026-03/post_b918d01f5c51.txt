📚 GS2 — Governance: Government Schemes and Initiatives

The Andhra Pradesh government has credited ₹2,670 crore to 46.85 lakh farmers as the third instalment of the PM Kisan-Annadata Sukhibhava programme. This initiative aims to improve farmers' income and modernise agriculture.

✅ KEY FACTS
• Andhra Pradesh government — PM Kisan-Annadata Sukhibhava programme: ₹2,670 crore credited to 46.85 lakh farmers.
• State government — Rythanna Meekosam programme: aims to support farmers through effective irrigation management.
• Chief Minister Chandrababu Naidu — Indian School of Agriculture: proposed to promote innovation and productivity in farming.
