The Andhra Pradesh government has credited ₹2,670 crore to the accounts of 46.85 lakh farmers towards the third instalment of the PM Kisan-Annadata Sukhibhava programme.

📚 GS3 — Economy: Agriculture and Rural Development

The Andhra Pradesh government has credited ₹2,670 crore to 46.85 lakh farmers under the PM Kisan-Annadata Sukhibhava programme. This initiative aims to improve farmers' income security and encourage agricultural productivity.

✅ KEY FACTS
• Andhra Pradesh government — PM Kisan-Annadata Sukhibhava programme: ₹2,670 crore credited to 46.85 lakh farmers
• Andhra Pradesh government — State's share: ₹1,874 crore
• Chief Minister N Chandrababu Naidu — Indian School of Agriculture: proposed to strengthen agriculture
