📚 GS2 — Governance: Government Schemes and Initiatives

The Kerala Cabinet has approved a new Film Policy to boost the film industry and promote tourism in the state. The policy aims to provide incentives to filmmakers and develop film infrastructure in the state.

✅ KEY FACTS
• Kerala Cabinet — Film Policy: Approved after discussions with stakeholders and the public.
• State Government — Film Incentives: Plans to provide incentives to filmmakers.
• State Government — Film Infrastructure: Plans to develop film infrastructure in the state.
