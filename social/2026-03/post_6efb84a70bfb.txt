📚 GS2 — Governance: Government Schemes and Initiatives

The Kerala Cabinet has approved a new Film Policy to promote the film industry in the state. The policy aims to increase film production and promote Kerala as a hub for film production.

✅ KEY FACTS
• Kerala Cabinet — Film Policy: Approved a new policy to promote the film industry in the state.
• Kerala Government — Film Production: Aims to increase film production in the state and promote Kerala as a hub for film production.
• Film Industry Stakeholders — Consultations: The policy was prepared after consultations with various stakeholders, including film producers, directors, and actors.
