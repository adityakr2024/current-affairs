📚 GS2 — Governance: Government Schemes and Initiatives

The Kerala Cabinet has approved a new Film Policy to promote the growth of the film industry in the state. The policy was prepared after consultations with various stakeholders, including those from the film industry. This move is expected to provide a boost to the film industry in Kerala, which has been facing several challenges in recent years.

✅ KEY FACTS
• Kerala Cabinet — Film Policy: Approved a new policy to promote the growth of the film industry in the state.
• State Government — Film Industry: Has been taking several initiatives to promote the film industry, including providing financial support to film producers.
