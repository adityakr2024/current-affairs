The A.P. Cabinet has approved ₹9,355 crore for seven Multi-Village Schemes to provide water and other facilities to over 65 lakh people.

📚 GS2 — Governance: Government Schemes and Initiatives

The A.P. Cabinet has approved ₹9,355 crore for seven Multi-Village Schemes to provide 55 litres of water per person per day to over 65 lakh people. The scheme also includes 30 ‘Sakhi Nivas’ hostels for single working women and widows.

✅ KEY FACTS
• A.P. Cabinet — Multi-Village Schemes: Approved ₹9,355 crore for seven schemes.
• A.P. Cabinet — Multi-Village Schemes: To provide 55 litres of water per person per day to over 65 lakh people.
• A.P. Cabinet — Sakhi Nivas: To construct 30 hostels for single working women, widows, and those living away from their families.
