📚 GS3 — Economy: Energy Security and Imports

India's LPG production has increased by 25% since the government issued supply maintenance orders. The country imports 60% of its LPG requirements, with 90% routed through the Strait of Hormuz.

✅ KEY FACTS
• Government — Supply Maintenance Orders: Implemented to increase LPG production by 25%.
• India — LPG Imports: 60% of requirements are imported, with 90% routed through the Strait of Hormuz.
• Government — Consumer Advisory: Urging consumers to refrain from panic booking to avoid disruptions in the supply chain.
