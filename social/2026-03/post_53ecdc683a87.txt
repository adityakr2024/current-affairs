📚 GS3 — Economy: Energy Security and Imports

India's LPG production has increased by 25% since supply maintenance orders were implemented. The country imports 60% of its LPG requirements, with 90% coming through the Strait of Hormuz. Officials are urging consumers to avoid panic booking.

✅ KEY FACTS
• Ministry of Petroleum and Natural Gas — LPG Production: Increased by 25% since supply maintenance orders.
• India — LPG Imports: 60% of total requirements, with 90% routed through the Strait of Hormuz.
• Government Officials — Consumer Advisory: Urging consumers to refrain from panic booking of LPG.
