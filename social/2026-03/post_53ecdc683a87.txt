📚 GS3 — Economy: Energy Security and Petroleum Sector

India's LPG production has increased by 25% since supply maintenance orders, reducing dependence on imports. The government urges people to refrain from panic booking to avoid unnecessary shortages.

✅ KEY FACTS
• The Ministry of Petroleum and Natural Gas — LPG Production: Increased by 25% since supply maintenance orders.
• The Ministry of Petroleum and Natural Gas — LPG Imports: 60% of the country's requirements are imported.
• The Ministry of Petroleum and Natural Gas — LPG Routing: 90% of LPG imports are routed through the Strait of Hormuz.
