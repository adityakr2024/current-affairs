📚 GS3 — Economy: Energy Security and LPG Production

India's LPG production has increased by 25% since supply maintenance orders, with 60% of the country's LPG requirements still being met through imports. Officials urge people to refrain from panic booking to avoid disruptions in the supply chain.

✅ KEY FACTS
• The Ministry of Petroleum and Natural Gas — LPG Production: Increased by 25% since supply maintenance orders.
• The Ministry of Petroleum and Natural Gas — LPG Imports: 60% of India's LPG requirements are met through imports.
• The Ministry of Petroleum and Natural Gas — Strait of Hormuz: 90% of India's LPG imports are routed through this shipping lane.
