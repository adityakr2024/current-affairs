The uptake of induction stoves has been sluggish in low-income Indian households, with only 5% of households having electric penetration in their kitchens.

📚 GS3 — Economy: Energy Security and Efficiency

Only 5% of Indian households have electric penetration in their kitchens, making it a significant barrier to the adoption of induction stoves. The Indian government needs to take further steps to promote clean energy and efficient cooking methods.
