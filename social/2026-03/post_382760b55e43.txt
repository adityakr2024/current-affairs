📚 GS3 — Economy: Energy Security and Trade

India's LPG production is 13 million tonnes, while consumption is 31 million tonnes, leading to a 60-40 import-export split. The country's lack of long-term storage facilities exacerbates the issue.

✅ KEY FACTS
• Government — LPG Production: 13 million tonnes in 2024-25.
• Government — LPG Consumption: 31 million tonnes in 2024-25.
• Government — Import-Export Split: 60-40.
