The ASEAN ministers are meeting to discuss the impact of the West Asia crisis on the region's economies. The crisis has led to surging oil prices and disruptions to trade flows, affecting the region's export-reliant economies.

✅ KEY FACTS
• ASEAN — Meeting: To address the West Asia crisis and its impact on the region's economies.
• ASEAN — Economy: The region's export-reliant economies are vulnerable to disruptions in shipping, logistics, and trade flows.
• ASEAN — Trade: The crisis has led to surging oil prices, affecting the region's trade flows.
