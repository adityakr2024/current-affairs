The ASEAN Ministers are holding meetings to address the West Asia crisis, focusing on surging oil prices and disruptions to shipping, logistics, and trade flows.

📚 GS2 — International Relations: Regional Organizations and Cooperation

The ASEAN Ministers are meeting to discuss the impacts of the West Asia crisis on the region's export-reliant economies. The crisis is expected to have significant economic implications, with surging oil prices and disruptions to shipping and trade flows.

✅ KEY FACTS
• ASEAN — Meeting: To address the West Asia crisis and its impacts on the region.
• The Philippines — ASEAN Chair: Hosting the meetings to discuss responses to the crisis.
• ASEAN — Export-reliant economies: Expected to be significantly affected by the surging oil prices and disruptions to trade flows.
