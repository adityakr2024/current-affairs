The ASEAN Ministers are holding meetings to address the West Asia crisis, which is impacting oil prices, shipping, logistics, and trade flows in the region.

📚 GS2 — International Relations: ASEAN and Regional Cooperation

The ASEAN Ministers are meeting to discuss the impact of the West Asia crisis on the region's economies. The crisis has led to surging oil prices and disruptions to shipping, logistics, and trade flows.

✅ KEY FACTS
• ASEAN — Meeting: To address the West Asia crisis and its impact on the region's economies.
• The Philippines — ASEAN Chair: Hosting the meetings to discuss the implications of the crisis.
• ASEAN Officials — Crisis Response: Weighing the impacts and responses to the crisis.
