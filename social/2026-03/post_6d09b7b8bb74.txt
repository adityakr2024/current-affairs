📌 WHY IN NEWS
ASEAN Ministers are holding meetings to address the West Asia crisis, which is impacting oil prices, shipping, logistics, and trade flows in the region.

📚 GS2 — International Relations: ASEAN and Regional Organizations

ASEAN Ministers are meeting to discuss the impact of the West Asia crisis on the region's economy. The crisis is affecting oil prices, shipping, and trade flows, and ASEAN member states are looking for ways to mitigate the impact.

✅ KEY FACTS
• ASEAN — Meeting: To discuss impacts and responses to surging oil prices and disruptions to shipping, logistics, and trade flows.
• The Philippines — ASEAN Chair: Hosting the meetings to coordinate the region’s response to the crisis.
• ASEAN — Economy: Heavily reliant on exports, with disruptions to trade flows causing concerns among officials.
