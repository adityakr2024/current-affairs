📌 WHY IN NEWS
ASEAN Ministers are holding meetings to address the West Asia crisis, focusing on surging oil prices and trade flow disruptions.

📚 GS2 — International Relations: ASEAN and Regional Cooperation

ASEAN Ministers are meeting to discuss the impact of the West Asia crisis on the region's export-reliant economies. The crisis has led to surging oil prices and disruptions to shipping and trade flows, posing a significant threat to the region's economic stability.

✅ KEY FACTS
• ASEAN — Meeting: To address West Asia crisis and surging oil prices.
• The Philippines — ASEAN Chair: Hosting the meetings to discuss impacts and responses.
• ASEAN — Export-reliant economies: Vulnerable to disruptions in global trade flows.
