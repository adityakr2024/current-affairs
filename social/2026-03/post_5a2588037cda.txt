📚 GS3 — Science & Tech: Education and Research

SRMIST has signed an MoU with the Naval Academy to collaborate in technical education and research. This partnership will provide opportunities for students and faculty to engage in joint research projects and share knowledge and expertise.

✅ KEY FACTS
• SRMIST — MoU: Signed with Naval Academy for technical education and research collaboration.
• SRMIST — Academic Activities: Exchange of faculty, students, and scholarly resources.
• SRMIST — Research Activities: Joint seminars, workshops, conferences, and expert lectures.
