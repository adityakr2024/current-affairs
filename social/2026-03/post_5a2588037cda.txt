📚 GS3 — Science & Tech: Education and Research

SRMIST has signed an MoU with the Naval Academy to collaborate in technical education and research. This partnership will provide opportunities for students and faculty to engage in joint research projects and exchange programs.

✅ KEY FACTS
• SRMIST — Naval Academy MoU: Collaboration in technical education and research.
• SRMIST — Naval Academy MoU: Exchange of faculty, students, and scholarly resources.
• SRMIST — Naval Academy MoU: Organisation of joint seminars, workshops, conferences, and expert lectures.
