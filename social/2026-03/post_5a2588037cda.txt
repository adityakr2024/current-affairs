📚 GS2 — Education: Institutional Collaborations and Research

SRMIST has signed an MoU with the Naval Academy to collaborate in technical education and research. This partnership will enhance the capabilities of both institutions and promote mutual understanding and cooperation.

✅ KEY FACTS
• SRMIST — Naval Academy MoU: Collaboration in technical education and research.
• SRMIST — Naval Academy: Exchange of faculty, students, and scholarly resources.
• SRMIST — Naval Academy: Organisation of joint seminars, workshops, conferences, and expert lectures.
