SRMIST has signed a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) with the Naval Academy to collaborate in technical education and research.

📚 GS2 — Education: Institutional Collaborations and Research

SRMIST and Naval Academy have signed an MoU to collaborate in technical education and research. This partnership will facilitate the sharing of knowledge, expertise, and resources, leading to mutual benefits and growth.

✅ KEY FACTS
• SRMIST — Naval Academy MoU: Collaborate in academic and research activities.
• SRMIST — Naval Academy MoU: Exchange of faculty, students, and scholarly resources.
• SRMIST — Naval Academy MoU: Organise joint seminars, workshops, conferences, and expert lectures.
