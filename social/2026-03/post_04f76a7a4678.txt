Progcap's ProgShakti initiative has facilitated over ₹10,000 crore of credit to women entrepreneurs, strengthening their credit profiles and contributing to the growth of the Indian economy. This initiative aims to provide easier access to credit for women-led businesses, promoting financial inclusion and women's entrepreneurship.
