📚 GS3 — Economy: Financial Inclusion and Women's Empowerment

Progcap's ProgShakti initiative has enabled over ₹10,000 crore in credit for women entrepreneurs, promoting financial inclusion and economic empowerment. This initiative has strengthened the credit profiles of women entrepreneurs, allowing them to access credit more easily.

✅ KEY FACTS
• Progcap — ProgShakti initiative: Enabled more than ₹10,000 crore of credit to women entrepreneurs.
• Progcap — ProgShakti initiative: Strengthened credit profiles of women entrepreneurs, promoting economic empowerment.
• Progcap — Financial Inclusion: Focused on providing financial support to underserved segments, including women entrepreneurs.
