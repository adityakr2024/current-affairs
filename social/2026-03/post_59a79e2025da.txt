📌 WHY IN NEWS
The Centre has termed reports of Iran allowing Indian ships through the Strait of Hormuz as premature, amidst concerns over the targeting of merchant navy ships in the region.

📚 GS2 — International Relations: India-Iran Bilateral

The Centre has expressed concern over the targeting of merchant navy ships in the Gulf and the Gulf of Oman near the Strait of Hormuz. Over 20% of the world's oil passes through the Strait, making it a critical waterway for international trade.

✅ KEY FACTS
• The Centre — Statement: Reports of Iran allowing Indian ships through the Strait of Hormuz are premature.
• The Indian Government — Diplomatic Efforts: Seeking assurances from Iran to ensure the safe passage of Indian ships.
• The Strait of Hormuz — Trade Importance: Over 20% of the world's oil passes through the Strait.
