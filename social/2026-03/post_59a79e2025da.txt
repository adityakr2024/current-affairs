📚 GS2 — International Relations: India-Iran Bilateral and Energy Security

The Centre has termed reports of Iran allowing Indian ships to pass through the Strait of Hormuz as premature. This comes amid concerns over the targeting of Indian merchant navy ships in the Gulf and the Gulf of Oman. The Strait of Hormuz is a critical waterway for India's energy security.

✅ KEY FACTS
• Centre — Statement: Reports of Iran allowing Indian ships through the Strait of Hormuz are premature.
• Government — Action: Expressing concern over the targeting of merchant navy ships in the Gulf and the Gulf of Oman.
• Strait of Hormuz — Importance: Critical waterway for India's energy security, with a significant portion of the country's oil imports passing through it.
