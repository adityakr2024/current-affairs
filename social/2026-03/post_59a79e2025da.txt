The Indian government has expressed concern about the safety of its ships in the Strait of Hormuz, with nearly 20% of the world's oil passing through the critical shipping lane. The Centre has termed reports of Iran allowing Indian ships through the Strait as premature.

• The Centre — Statement: Reports of Iran allowing Indian ships through the Strait of Hormuz are premature.
• The Indian Government — Talks: India has been in talks with Iran to ensure the safe passage of its ships.
• The Indian Navy — Deployment: The Indian Navy has been deployed in the region to provide security to Indian ships.
