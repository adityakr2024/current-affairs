📚 GS2 — Polity: Parliament and Legislation

The Cabinet has cleared amendments to the Insolvency and Bankruptcy Code and the Companies Act, 2013. This is expected to improve the efficiency of the insolvency resolution process and provide a better regulatory framework for companies in India.

✅ KEY FACTS
• Cabinet — Insolvency and Bankruptcy Code: Cleared amendments to improve efficiency.
• Cabinet — Companies Act, 2013: Cleared amendments to improve regulatory framework.
• Ministry of Corporate Affairs — Insolvency and Bankruptcy Code: Will implement the amendments.
