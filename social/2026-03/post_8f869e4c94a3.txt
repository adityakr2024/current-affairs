📚 GS3 — Economy: Corporate Governance and Insolvency

The Cabinet has approved amendments to the Insolvency and Bankruptcy Code and the Companies Act, 2013. The move is expected to improve the insolvency resolution process and provide more clarity on the rights of creditors.

✅ KEY FACTS
• Cabinet — Insolvency and Bankruptcy Code: Cleared amendments to improve insolvency resolution process.
• Cabinet — Companies Act, 2013: Approved amendments to provide more clarity on the rights of creditors.
• Ministry of Corporate Affairs — Insolvency and Bankruptcy Code: Will implement the amendments to improve the corporate sector.
