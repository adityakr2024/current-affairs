SRMIST signs MoU with Naval Academy to collaborate in technical education and research

एसआरएमआईएसटी और नेवल अकादमी ने तकनीकी शिक्षा और अनुसंधान में सहयोग के लिए समझौता ज्ञापन पर हस्ताक्षर किए

Context

The SRM Institute of Science and Technology (SRMIST) has signed a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) with the Naval Academy to collaborate in technical education and research. The institutions will work together in academic and research activities, including exchange of faculty, students, and scholarly resources. They will also organise joint seminars, workshops, conferences, and expert lectures. This collaboration aims to promote mutual understanding and cooperation between the two institutions. The MoU will facilitate the sharing of knowledge, expertise, and resources.

Background

The SRMIST has been actively engaged in collaborative research and academic activities with various institutions in the past. The Naval Academy has also been involved in similar collaborations to enhance its technical education and research capabilities.

Key Points
  • SRMIST — Naval Academy MoU: Collaborate in academic and research activities.
  • SRMIST — Naval Academy MoU: Exchange of faculty, students, and scholarly resources.
  • SRMIST — Naval Academy MoU: Organise joint seminars, workshops, conferences, and expert lectures.
  • SRMIST — Naval Academy MoU: Promote mutual understanding and cooperation.
  • SRMIST — Naval Academy MoU: Facilitate sharing of knowledge, expertise, and resources.
Implication

This collaboration is expected to enhance the technical education and research capabilities of both institutions. It will also promote the sharing of knowledge, expertise, and resources, leading to mutual benefits and growth.
