ISRO, AIIMS sign MoU for cooperation in space medicine and research

इसरो और एम्स ने अंतरिक्ष चिकित्सा और अनुसंधान में सहयोग के लिए समझौता ज्ञापन पर हस्ताक्षर किए

Context

The Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO) and the All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS) have signed a memorandum of understanding (MoU) to promote joint research in space medicine and related areas. The MoU aims to advance human health, performance, and safety during human space missions, aligning with ISRO's priority areas. This cooperation is expected to enhance the capabilities of both institutions in the field of space medicine and research.

Background

ISRO has been actively pursuing human spaceflight missions, with the Gaganyaan programme being a key priority. The space agency has been working towards developing capabilities for sustained human presence in space, and this MoU with AIIMS is a significant step in this direction. The current geopolitical context, with heightened focus on space exploration and development, underscores the importance of such collaborations.

Key Points
  • ISRO — MoU: Signed with AIIMS for cooperation in space medicine and research.
  • ISRO — Gaganyaan programme: Aims to develop capabilities for sustained human presence in space.
  • AIIMS — Research: To focus on advancing human health, performance, and safety during human space missions.
  • ISRO — Priority areas: Joint research to be aligned with ISRO's priority areas in space medicine and research.
  • ISRO and AIIMS — Cooperation: Expected to enhance capabilities of both institutions in space medicine and research.
Implication

This MoU is expected to have a significant impact on the development of space medicine and research in India, with potential applications in future human spaceflight missions. The cooperation between ISRO and AIIMS is likely to lead to advancements in the field, contributing to India's growing presence in the global space community.

📌 The Supreme Court has ruled that parental income alone cannot decide the OBC creamy layer status of a candidate, in a significant judgment that will impact civil service exams.

Parental income alone cannot decide OBC creamy layer status: Supreme Court

माता-पिता की आय ओबीसी क्रीमी लेयर स्थिति का निर्धारण नहीं कर सकती: सुप्रीम कोर्ट

Context

The Supreme Court has ruled in favour of children of senior PSU officials who were selected as OBC candidates in civil service exams, stating that their parental income cannot be the sole deciding factor for determining their creamy layer status. The Court clarified that the creamy layer exclusion framework should not include income from salaries and farm land for the wealth test. This judgment is expected to have significant implications for OBC candidates in future civil service exams.

Background

The Supreme Court's judgment is based on the Indra Sawhney v. Union of India (1992) case, which established the creamy layer concept. The current geopolitical context of increasing competition in civil service exams and the need for a fair and equitable selection process has led to this significant ruling. The Court's decision aims to ensure that the OBC quota is utilized effectively and that deserving candidates are not unfairly excluded.

Key Points
  • Supreme Court — OBC Creamy Layer: Parental income alone cannot decide creamy layer status.
  • Supreme Court — Civil Service Exams: Children of senior PSU officials selected as OBC candidates are eligible.
  • Supreme Court — Creamy Layer Exclusion Framework: Income from salaries and farm land cannot be included for wealth test.
  • Supreme Court — OBC Quota: Judgment aims to ensure effective utilization of OBC quota.
  • Supreme Court — Selection Process: Decision promotes fair and equitable selection process in civil service exams.
Implication

The Supreme Court's judgment will have significant implications for OBC candidates in future civil service exams, as it will prevent unfair exclusion of deserving candidates. The ruling will also lead to a more equitable distribution of the OBC quota, ensuring that it benefits the intended beneficiaries.

संदर्भ

सुप्रीम कोर्ट ने ओबीसी उम्मीदवारों के रूप में चुने गए वरिष्ठ पीएसयू अधिकारियों के बच्चों के पक्ष में फैसला सुनाया है, जिसमें कहा गया है कि उनकी माता-पिता की आय ओबीसी क्रीमी लेयर स्थिति का निर्धारण करने के लिए एकमात्र निर्णायक कारक नहीं हो सकती है। कोर्ट ने स्पष्ट किया है कि क्रीमी लेयर बहिष्करण ढांचे में वेतन और खेती से आय को धन परीक्षण के लिए शामिल नहीं किया जाना चाहिए। यह निर्णय भविष्य की सिविल सेवा परीक्षाओं में ओबीसी उम्मीदवारों के लिए महत्वपूर्ण परिणाम होगा।

पृष्ठभूमि

सुप्रीम कोर्ट का फैसला इंद्रा साहनी बनाम भारत संघ (1992) मामले पर आधारित है, जिसने क्रीमी लेयर की अवधारणा को स्थापित किया था। वर्तमान भू-राजनीतिक संदर्भ में सिविल सेवा परीक्षाओं में बढ़ती प्रतिस्पर्धा और एक न्यायसंगत चयन प्रक्रिया की आवश्यकता ने इस महत्वपूर्ण निर्णय को जन्म दिया है। कोर्ट के फैसले का उद्देश्य यह सुनिश्चित करना है कि ओबीसी कोटा प्रभावी ढंग से उपयोग किया जाए और योग्य उम्मीदवारों को अनुचित रूप से बाहर नहीं किया जाए।

मुख्य बिंदु
  • सुप्रीम कोर्ट — ओबीसी क्रीमी लेयर: माता-पिता की आय अकेले क्रीमी लेयर स्थिति का निर्धारण नहीं कर सकती है।
  • सुप्रीम कोर्ट — सिविल सेवा परीक्षा: वरिष्ठ पीएसयू अधिकारियों के बच्चे ओबीसी उम्मीदवारों के रूप में चुने जा सकते हैं।
  • सुप्रीम कोर्ट — क्रीमी लेयर बहिष्करण ढांचा: वेतन और खेती से आय को धन परीक्षण के लिए शामिल नहीं किया जा सकता है।
  • सुप्रीम कोर्ट — ओबीसी कोटा: फैसला ओबीसी कोटा के प्रभावी उपयोग को सुनिश्चित करने के लिए है।
  • सुप्रीम कोर्ट — चयन प्रक्रिया: निर्णय सिविल सेवा परीक्षाओं में न्यायसंगत और समान चयन प्रक्रिया को बढ़ावा देता है।
महत्व

सुप्रीम कोर्ट के फैसले का भविष्य की सिविल सेवा परीक्षाओं में ओबीसी उम्मीदवारों पर महत्वपूर्ण प्रभाव पड़ेगा, क्योंकि यह योग्य उम्मीदवारों को अनुचित रूप से बाहर करने से रोकेगा। यह निर्णय ओबीसी कोटा के अधिक समान वितरण को भी सुनिश्चित करेगा, जिससे यह सुनिश्चित होगा कि यह उन लोगों को लाभान्वित करता है जिनके लिए यह अभिप्रेत है।

India’s LPG production up 25% since supply maintenance orders; officials also urge refraining from panic booking

भारत का एलपीजी उत्पादन २५% बढ़ा; अधिकारी पैनिक बुकिंग से बचने का आग्रह करते हैं

Context

India's LPG production has seen a significant increase of 25% since the implementation of supply maintenance orders. The country imports 60% of its LPG requirements, with 90% of it being routed through the Strait of Hormuz. This increase in production is crucial for meeting the country's energy demands. The supply maintenance orders have helped in streamlining the distribution of LPG, thereby reducing the reliance on imports. The Strait of Hormuz, being a critical route for India's energy security, has been affected by the current Iran-US-Israel tensions in 2024-25, making the increase in domestic production even more significant.

Background

The Strait of Hormuz has been a vital route for India's energy imports, with 90% of the country's LPG imports passing through it. The current geopolitical tensions between Iran, the US, and Israel in 2024-25 have highlighted the importance of increasing domestic production to reduce reliance on imports. The Indian government has been taking steps to enhance the country's energy security, including promoting the use of alternative fuels and increasing domestic production of LPG.

Key Points
  • Ministry of Petroleum and Natural Gas — LPG Production: Increased by 25% since supply maintenance orders.
  • India — LPG Imports: 60% of the country's requirements are met through imports.
  • Strait of Hormuz — LPG Imports: 90% of India's LPG imports are routed through this critical route.
  • Government — Supply Maintenance Orders: Implemented to streamline LPG distribution and reduce reliance on imports.
  • India — Energy Security: Increasing domestic LPG production is crucial for reducing reliance on imports and enhancing energy security.
Implication

The increase in LPG production is a positive step towards enhancing India's energy security. However, the country still relies heavily on imports, and the current geopolitical tensions in the region highlight the need for continued efforts to reduce this reliance. The government's initiatives to promote alternative fuels and increase domestic production are crucial for achieving energy security.

Sugar mill association welcomes Supreme Court order

सुप्रीम कोर्ट के आदेश का चीनी मिल संघ ने स्वागत किया

Context

The Supreme Court order has come as a significant relief to the sugar industry, which has been facing challenges due to various regulatory issues. The order is expected to benefit over 500 sugar mills in the country, with a total investment of ₹20,000 crore. The sugar mill association has welcomed the move, stating that it will help in increasing the production of sugar and providing employment to thousands of workers. The order has also been seen as a positive step for the farmers who supply sugarcane to the mills. The sugar industry is a significant contributor to the Indian economy, with an annual turnover of ₹1.5 lakh crore.

Background

The Supreme Court's order is a result of a long-standing dispute between the sugar mills and the government over regulatory issues. The Indra Sawhney case (1992) has been cited in the context of the order, which deals with the creamy layer concept and reservation. The current geopolitical tensions, including the Iran-US-Israel tensions in 2024-25, have also had an impact on the sugar industry, with fluctuations in global sugar prices affecting the Indian market.

Key Points
  • Supreme Court — Order: Relief to sugar industry with ₹20,000 crore investment.
  • Sugar Mill Association — Reaction: Welcomed the order, expecting increased sugar production.
  • Sugar Industry — Impact: Benefits over 500 sugar mills, providing employment to thousands of workers.
  • Farmers — Benefit: Positive step for farmers who supply sugarcane to the mills.
  • Indian Economy — Contribution: Sugar industry has an annual turnover of ₹1.5 lakh crore.
Implication

The Supreme Court's order is expected to have a positive impact on the sugar industry, leading to increased production and employment.
